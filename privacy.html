OptiAI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2025

OptiAI SEO Booster does not access or store any customer personal information (names, emails, addresses, orders, or payment details).

1. Data We Collect

To provide SEO intelligence, OptiAI accesses the following data via the Shopify API:

  • Product Information: Titles, descriptions, images, and prices to perform SEO audits and generate test data.
  • Shop Details: Store name and domain to personalize the app experience.
  • Usage Data: Statistics on which AI variations you deploy to improve our suggestion engine (anonymized).

2. How We Use AI (Grok-beta)

OptiAI uses xAI’s Grok-beta model to generate SEO strategies. When you run a "Spy" or "Audit":

  • Selected product content is sent to xAI for real-time analysis.
  • No Training: We do not allow xAI to use your store's proprietary data to train their general models.
  • Data sent to the AI is transient and used only to generate your specific results.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your SEO audit history as long as the app is installed. If you uninstall OptiAI, all stored data related to your shop is automatically deleted from our database within 48 hours, in compliance with Shopify’s Mandatory Webhooks.

4. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect data in transit between your Shopify store, our servers, and the xAI API.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new terms.

6. Contact Us

For questions regarding your data, please contact our support team through the Support tab in the app or via the Shopify Partner Dashboard.
